Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 March 2026  ·  STAR AI Tutor  ·  Northern Ireland, United Kingdom

⚠️ This policy has been drafted in good faith but has not been reviewed by a solicitor. We recommend obtaining independent legal advice before launching to paying customers, particularly regarding UK GDPR compliance and children's data obligations under the Age Appropriate Design Code.

1. Who We Are

STAR AI Tutor is an AI-powered tutoring platform for P6 and P7 pupils in Northern Ireland preparing for the SEAG Transfer Test. The service is operated by Chris Gregg, trading as STAR AI Tutor, based in Northern Ireland, United Kingdom ("we", "us", "our").

We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@star-tutor.co.uk.

2. What Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Email address (used to create and access your account)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your actual password)
  • Child's first name (used to personalise the learning experience)
  • Year group (P6 or P7)
  • Parent or guardian email address
  • Intended exam year and preferred number of study sessions per week

Learning Data

  • Results from practice sessions, topic sprints, and mock exams (scores, correct/incorrect answers per question, topics attempted)
  • Session timestamps and duration
  • Topic performance summaries used to personalise recommendations

Payment Data

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your card number, expiry date, or CVV. We receive a subscription status (active, trialing, inactive) and a Stripe customer identifier from Stripe to manage your access. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/gb/privacy.

Usage Data

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised data about how visitors use our website, including pages visited, time on site, and device type. This data is aggregated and does not identify individuals. See Section 8 (Cookies) for more detail.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service: delivering personalised practice sessions, tracking progress, and displaying results to the student and parent.
  • To personalise learning: using topic performance data to recommend which areas to focus on.
  • To manage your subscription: processing payments via Stripe and controlling access based on subscription status.
  • To generate practice questions: your year group and topic performance data are used when constructing prompts sent to our AI question generator. No personal identifying information (name, email) is included in these prompts.
  • To communicate with you: sending account confirmation emails and, where you have opted in, service updates. We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To improve the service: using aggregated, anonymised analytics data to understand how the platform is used and where improvements are needed.

Our legal basis for processing personal data is contract performance (to deliver the service you have subscribed to) and, where applicable, legitimate interests (to improve the service and prevent fraud). For children's data, our legal basis is parental consent given at account creation.

4. AI-Generated Content

Practice questions, hints, and feedback on this platform are generated using Claude, an AI model developed by Anthropic, PBC. When generating questions, we send the following information to Anthropic's API: the year group (P6 or P7), the topic area, and the question format required. We do not send your name, email address, or any other personal identifying information.

Anthropic processes this data as a data processor acting on our behalf. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

AI-generated content is reviewed against a set of validation rules before being shown to students. However, AI systems can make errors. We strongly recommend that parents review any question or explanation that seems incorrect and contact us so we can investigate. AI-generated content on STAR AI Tutor is not a substitute for qualified professional tutoring.

5. Children's Privacy

STAR AI Tutor is designed for use by children aged 9–12, under the supervision and with the consent of a parent or guardian. The account must be created by a parent or guardian, not the child.

We comply with the UK Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code). We do not use children's data for profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose beyond delivering and improving the educational service. We do not share children's personal data with any third party except those listed in Section 6 for the purpose of delivering the service.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, please contact us immediately at support@star-tutor.co.uk and we will delete it promptly.

6. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with the following third-party service providers, solely for the purpose of delivering this service:

  • Supabase, Inc. (database and authentication) — your account data and learning records are stored in Supabase's cloud database, hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU (Ireland). Supabase is ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2 Type II compliant.
  • Stripe, Inc. (payment processing) — processes subscription payments. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.
  • Anthropic, PBC (AI question generation) — receives anonymised question generation prompts (year group and topic only, no personal data). Anthropic does not use API inputs to train its models by default.
  • Vercel, Inc. (website hosting) — hosts the STAR AI Tutor website and serverless functions on EU-region infrastructure.
  • Google LLC (analytics) — receives anonymised, aggregated usage data via Google Analytics 4. IP addresses are anonymised before transmission.

All third-party providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you cancel your subscription, your account data and learning records are retained for 12 months to allow reactivation. After 12 months of inactivity, we will delete or anonymise your data.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@star-tutor.co.uk. We will action deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use the following cookies on this website:

  • Authentication cookies (Supabase) — strictly necessary, used to keep you logged in. These cannot be disabled without preventing the service from functioning.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4) — used to understand how visitors use our website. These are set only with your consent where required by law.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not use cookies to build profiles for commercial purposes.

9. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — you may ask us to delete your personal data (subject to certain legal exceptions).
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — you may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@star-tutor.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will notify active subscribers of material changes by email. Continued use of the service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
